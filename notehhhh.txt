NCF ENROLLMENT PORTAL -  DOCUMENTATION

BACKEND 

Framework: Django 5.0
Database: SQLite3
Architecture: Model-View-Template (MVT)

DATABASE MODELS (5)


1. UserProfile
   - user: OneToOneField(User)
   - role: CharField (admin, staff, user)

2. Department
   - name, code, head, email, created_at

3. Course
   - name, code, department, credits, capacity, description, created_at

4. Student
   - name, student_number, email, phone, department, gender, date_of_birth, address
   - created_at, updated_at

5. Enrollment
   - student, course, status, grade, enrolled_date
   - Unique constraint: (student, course)


RELATIONSHIPS

Department (1) -> (Many) Course
Department (1) -> (Many) Student
Student (Many) <-> (Many) Course (through Enrollment)

AUTHORIZATION

Decorators:
- @login_required: All protected views
- @admin_required: Delete operations, user management, department management, course management
- @staff_required: Student create/edit, enrollment management

Role Hierarchy:
- Admin: Full access (departments, courses, students, enrollments, users)
- Staff: Student and enrollment management only
- User: Read-only access
