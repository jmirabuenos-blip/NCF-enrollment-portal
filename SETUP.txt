ROLE DIFFERENCES

ADMIN:
- Create, edit, delete departments
- Create, edit, delete courses
- Create, edit, delete students
- View and manage all enrollments
- Manage user roles
- Full system access

STAFF:
- Create, edit students
- View and manage all enrollments
- View courses and departments
- Cannot manage courses or departments
- Cannot delete anything

USER (STUDENTS):
- Create their own student profile
- Edit their own profile
- Choose their department
- Enroll in available courses
- View their enrollments
- Cancel pending enrollments
- View available courses
- Cannot see other students' data

HOW TO USE AS A STUDENT (USER ROLE)

1. Register an account
2. Login with your credentials
3. Click "Create Profile" in sidebar
4. Fill in your information:
   - Name
   - Student Number (e.g., 24-04804)
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Choose your department
   - Gender, date of birth, address
5. Click "Available Courses" to see courses
6. Click "My Enrollments" then "Enroll in Course"
7. Select a course and click "Enroll"
8. Your enrollment will be in "Pending" status
9. You can cancel pending enrollments anytime
10. Once approved by staff/admin, status changes to "Enrolled"
