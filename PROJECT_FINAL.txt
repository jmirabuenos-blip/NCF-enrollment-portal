USER FEATURES

1. CREATE STUDENT PROFILE:
   - Users can create their own student profile
   - Choose their department
   - Fill in personal information
   - Student number format: 24-04804

2. ENROLL IN COURSES:
   - View available courses
   - Enroll in courses they want
   - Enrollment starts as "Pending"
   - Staff/Admin can approve to "Enrolled"

3. MANAGE OWN ENROLLMENTS:
   - View their enrollments
   - Cancel pending enrollments
   - Cannot cancel enrolled/completed courses
   - See enrollment status (Pending, Enrolled, Completed, Dropped)

4. EDIT OWN PROFILE:
   - Update their information
   - Change department
   - Update contact details
   - Cannot edit other students

5. VIEW AVAILABLE COURSES:
   - See all courses offered
   - Check course details
   - Decide which to enroll in

ENROLLMENT STATUSES

PENDING: Just enrolled, waiting for approval
ENROLLED: Approved by staff/admin, actively taking course
COMPLETED: Finished the course
DROPPED: Cancelled or withdrawn

Users can only cancel PENDING enrollments!

HOW TO USE AS A STUDENT
=======================

1. Register an account at http://localhost:8000/register/
2. Login with your credentials
3. Click "Create Profile" in sidebar
4. Fill in your information and choose department
5. Click "Available Courses" to browse courses
6. Click "My Enrollments" then "Enroll in Course"
7. Select a course and click "Enroll"
8. Your enrollment will be "Pending"
9. Wait for staff/admin to approve
10. You can cancel pending enrollments anytime
